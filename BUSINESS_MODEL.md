# Align Sustainability Model

Align is a free, open-source, local-first app. Any commercial work around it should add optional value without making the core app worse for free users.

## Principles

- Keep the app fully useful without payment.
- Do not make public users depend on the maintainer's private backend.
- Keep paid assets separate from the source repository unless they are intentionally published as free samples.
- Never include real client data, credentials, private notes, or customer content in public templates or docs.

## Optional Revenue Paths

- Workflow template packs for repeatable project and planning setups.
- Setup or customization services for users who want help configuring their own workflow.
- A future hosted cloud service only if there is enough demand to justify managed sync, reminders, and backups.

## What Stays Free

- Local-first web and desktop app.
- Projects, tasks, todos, calendar, notes, reports, and full backup/import.
- Self-hosting documentation.
- Public sample templates.
- Source code.

## Public Repo Rule

This repository should describe the sustainability model at a high level only. Internal pricing tests, customer notes, and operational plans should stay outside the public repo.
